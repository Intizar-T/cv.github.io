Title: Covalent Amine Tethering on Ketone
Modified Porous Organic Polymers for
Enhanced CO2 Capture
Authors: P. Jorayev, I. Tashov, V. Rozyyev, T. S. Nguyen
N. A. Dogan, C. T. Yavuz*
Description: Chemicals that are utilized to capture CO2
industrially degrade during the process due
to their instability. We took those CO2 "loving"
chemicals and attached them to a solid nano
network (porous polymer) to assure they don't
get lost during the capture process. By doing
that, we increased the capture capacity of the
material dramatically.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1002/cssc.202002190



Title: Lewis acid catalyzed hydroxylation of
triphenylmethane.
Authors: P. Jorayev, I. Tashov, V. Rozyyev, C. T. Yavuz*
Description: The project uses cheap Lewis acids
to accomplish a very hard task, which is
breaking a C-H bond that doesn't have any
highly electronegative functional groups and
attach a functional group that further can
be modified to other useful chemicals. The process
achieves 100% conversion and yield in a single step
reaction. The separation process is also very simple.


DOI: Expected to be published in 2021
